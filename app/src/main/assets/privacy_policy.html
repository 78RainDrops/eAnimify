Application: eAnimify: ANDROID ENDEMIC ANIMAL IDENTIFICATION AND INFORMATION GUIDE WITH REVERSE IMAGE SEARCH

Operational and Security Policy

Effective Date: November 2024

Policy Statement:

eAnimify is dedicated to protect the information assets and user data within its application, ensuring the protection of endemic animal data, user privacy, and security. This security policy outlines the principles, practices, and responsibilities aimed at protecting our application and its ecosystem from unauthorized access.

Scope:

This policy applies to the developers and users (DENR, wildlife enthusiast and conservationist) that use the eAnimify application.

Security Objectives:

1. Ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of user data and endemic animal information.
2. Protection against unauthorized access and data breaches.
3. Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards
4. Promote a culture of security awareness and accountability among users.

Responsibilities:

Development Team:

• Maintain the security control of the application.
• Regularly update and patch the system to address vulnerabilities.

Users:

• Use strong password and maintain the confidentiality of login credentials.
• Report any suspicious activity or app malfunctions promptly.

Data Protection:

• All of the user’s data is encrypted.
• Regular data backups are conducted and stored securely.

Incident Response:

• All security incidents must be reported to the development team immediately.
• An incident response plan is in place for the investigation, containment and resolution of incidents.
• After an incident, a thorough review will be conducted to determine the cause and prevent future occurrences by improving security practices.

Training and Awareness:

• Users will undergo regular training on emerging security threats.
• Users will be educated on best practices for safe application use and data privacy.

Enforcement:

• Security and operational incidents are documented for accountability and continuous improvement.

Compliance:

eAnimify complies with data protection regulations and software quality standards, including ISO 25010.

Review and Revision:

This security policy will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to address emerging threats and changes in the app’s usage environment.
